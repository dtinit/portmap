Query the Portability Map

Do you need to move your online data to a new home? It can be hard to find out how to do so, whether there's really no good way, one good way or even several different approaches with different tradeoffs.

The Data Transfer Initiative is interested in making an improvement in how you can move your own data, and as part of that, this site offers a way to query a library of possible solutions.
